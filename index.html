Manufacturing mentality

I know it sounds funny, but it makes sense I promise.

Manufacturing generally involves a very fast passed environment where ideas must be put into actions in a quick, precise and safe way. For me, there's a lot involved in the definition of this simple word.

From planning, engineering, designing every part of a process to delivering precise instructions and materials so the last person involved carries on its job no matter how big or small.

My artistic vision portraits it like orquestrating a beautifull play where everyone has to execute an specific task properly and on time. It is defining, training, communicating, coordinating, and monitoring the group as a whole.

And me? Well I've developed this view where I'm the director, a player and even the expectator. Putting myself out there in their shoes while I'm "over here" writing the score and shooting the spotlight over the company so its customers applaud such tireless performance.
